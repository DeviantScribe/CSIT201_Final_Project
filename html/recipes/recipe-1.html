Mrs. Field's Chocolate
Chip Cookies

If you've always wanted to recreate Mrs. Fields
cookies at home, this recipe works beautifully.

YIELD: 24 COOKIES (1 RECIPE) / 48 COOKIES (2 RECIPES)

Ingredients 1 Recipe 2 Recipes
butter, softened 1 cup (2 sticks) 2 cups (4 sticks)
granulated sugar 1/2 cup 1 cup
packed light brown sugar 1 1/2 cups 3 cups
large eggs 2 4
vanilla extract 2 1/2 teaspoons 5 teaspoons
all-purpose flour 2 1/2 cups 5 cups
salt 3/4 teaspoon 1 1/2 teaspoons
baking powder 1 teaspoon 2 teaspoons
baking soda 1 teaspoon 2 teaspoons
semisweet chocolate chips 1 1/2 twelve-ounce bags (18 ounces) 3 twelve-ounce bags (36 ounces)

PROCEDURE:
  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.

  2. In a large mixing bowl, cream together the butter, sugars, eggs, and vanilla.

  3. In another bowl, mix together the flour, salt, baking baking powder and baking soda.

  4. Combine the wet and dry ingredients.

  5. Stir in the chocolate chips.

  6. With your fingers, place golf ball-sized dough portions 2 inches apart on an ungreased cookie sheet.

  7. Bake for 9 to 10 minutes or just until the edges are light brown.
